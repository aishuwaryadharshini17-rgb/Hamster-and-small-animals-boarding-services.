About Cozy Critters Stay

A calm boarding service built and designed for tiny pets

We focus on the things that matter most for tiny companions: tidy habitats, quiet rest periods, careful observation, and thoughtful enrichment. That means every guest gets a boarding experience that feels warm, structured, and easy to trust.

Each stay is arranged to feel calm from the first check-in to bedtime, with consistent feeding notes, neat suite preparation, and update-friendly care moments that help owners feel reassured while they are away.

  • Clean environment Habitats are refreshed with organised bedding, food zones, and water checks.
  • Gentle handling Interactions are calm and patient to reduce stress for delicate, easily startled pets.
  • Small-pet experience Care routines are tailored for tiny companions rather than treated like a one-size-fits-all setup.
What owners can expect

Clear care notes, thoughtful boarding routines, and a service style that feels warm, clean, and dependable.
