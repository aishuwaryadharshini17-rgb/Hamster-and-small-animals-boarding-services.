Helpful Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

These are the questions owners ask most often when arranging dates for a hamster, rabbit, guinea pig, or another small companion.

How do I check whether my dates are still open?
Review the live schedule above, then submit your preferred date range. We confirm the best open option and suggest alternatives if your first choice is nearly full.
What should I bring at drop-off?
Bring your pet's usual food, familiar bedding, medication if needed, and any care instructions that help us keep routines steady and low-stress.
What happens if my requested dates are already full?
We can place your request on the waitlist, guide you toward the next calm opening, and contact you in request order if a space becomes available.
Will I receive updates while my pet is staying?
Yes. We send gentle, clear updates so you know how your pet is settling, eating, resting, and adjusting throughout the stay.
Need a quicker answer?

Talk to our care team before you lock in dates.

We can help you choose the right suite, explain the waitlist, and guide you toward the smoothest option for your pet.
