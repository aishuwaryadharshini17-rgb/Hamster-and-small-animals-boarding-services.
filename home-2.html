Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers before you book.

These are the questions owners usually ask first when arranging a stay for a hamster, rabbit, guinea pig, or another small companion.

How do I book a stay for my pet?
Share your dates, pet type, and any comfort notes using the form above. We then reply with availability, care expectations, and the next simple steps for confirming the stay.
What should I bring at drop-off?
Bring your pet's usual food, any important care instructions, and comfort items that help the enclosure feel familiar and low-stress from the start.
How do you keep small pets calm and comfortable?
We follow soft handling, tidy habitats, temperature-aware placement, and daily routine checks so little companions settle gently instead of feeling overstimulated.
Will I receive updates during the stay?
Yes. Owners receive warm, clear updates about settling, eating, rest, and general comfort so you never feel left guessing about how the stay is going.
